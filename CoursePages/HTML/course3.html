Course Goals
  • 1. Earth Resources:
    • Understand the concept of Earth's resources and their importance.
    • Identify different types of Earth resources, such as water, air, soil, and minerals.
    • Learn about responsible usage and conservation of Earth's resources.
  • 2. Earth Landforms:
    • Explore various landforms on Earth, including mountains, valleys, plains, and bodies of water.
    • Understand the processes that shape these landforms, such as erosion and weathering.
    • Identify and describe different landforms on maps and in real-world examples.
  • 3. Earth Weather and Climate:
    • Comprehend the difference between weather and climate.
    • Explore the elements of weather, including temperature, precipitation, and wind.
    • Understand the factors influencing climate and how it varies in different regions.
  • 4. Classifying Organisms:
    • Learn the basics of biological classification.
    • Understand the importance of categorizing living organisms.
    • Classify common organisms based on their characteristics.
  • 5. Food Chains:
    • Explore the concept of food chains and the interdependence of living organisms.
    • Identify producers, consumers, and decomposers in a food chain.
    • Understand the flow of energy through different trophic levels.
  • 6. How Living Things Change:
    • Explore life cycles of various organisms, including plants and animals.
    • Understand the concept of metamorphosis and growth stages.
    • Recognize the importance of adaptation and evolution in living organisms.
  • 7. Earth's Rotation & Revolution:
    • Understand the Earth's rotation and its impact on day and night.
    • Explore the concept of Earth's revolution around the Sun and its effect on seasons.
    • Demonstrate an understanding of the Earth's axial tilt.
  • 8. How Orbits in Space Work:
    • Learn about the orbits of planets and other celestial bodies in the solar system.
    • Understand the gravitational forces that govern orbits.
    • Explore the characteristics of planetary orbits.
  • 9. Weather In Space (the Rocky Planets):
    • Investigate the atmospheric conditions and weather patterns on rocky planets.
    • Understand the factors influencing weather in space.
    • Compare and contrast the weather conditions on different rocky planets in our solar system.
