Course Goals
  • 1. Mastering Basic Addition:
    • Develop fluency in easy and simple addition.
    • Understand the concept of remainder in addition problems.
    • Apply addition skills to solve real-life problems.
  • 2. Understanding Place Value:
    • Learn the concept of units, tens, and hundreds in numbers.
    • Apply place value understanding to read and write numbers effectively.
  • 3. Proficient Subtraction:
    • Gain proficiency in simple and remainder subtraction.
    • Apply subtraction skills to solve mathematical problems in various contexts.
  • 4. Introduction to Multiplication:
    • Understand the basics of multiplication.
    • Learn how to multiply using easy and simple methods.
    • Apply multiplication skills to solve practical problems.
  • 5. Learning Division:
    • Grasp the concept of division and its relationship with multiplication.
    • Develop proficiency in easy and simple division.
    • Understand and apply remainder concepts in division.
  • 6. Exploring Measurement: The Meter:
    • Learn about longitude and the unit of length, the meter.
    • Understand the importance of measurement in everyday life.
    • Apply knowledge of meters to measure and compare lengths.
  • 7. Discovering Circles and Circumference:
    • Explore the concept of circles and their properties.
    • Understand the concept of circumference and how to calculate it.
    • Apply knowledge of circles and circumference in practical scenarios.
  • 8. Identifying Polygons:
    • Learn about different types of polygons.
    • Recognize and distinguish between various polygon shapes.
    • Apply knowledge of polygons in geometry problems.
  • 9. Understanding Triangle Types:
    • Explore different types of triangles.
    • Recognize and classify triangles based on their angles and sides.
    • Apply knowledge of triangles in geometry and problem-solving.
  • 10. Application of Mathematical Concepts:
    • Apply learned mathematical concepts in real-world scenarios.
    • Engage in activities and exercises that reinforce understanding and problem-solving skills.
